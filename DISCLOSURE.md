# AI Assistance Disclosure Policy

This repository follows a clear and consistent policy for disclosing the use of AI-assisted tools in the preparation of research artifacts.

## Scope

This policy applies to all materials in this repository, including:
- Research papers
- Appendices
- Briefs (executive, board-level)
- Diagrams and figures
- Repository documentation

This research was produced with significant AI assistance across the analysis and synthesis pipeline:

- **ChatGPT**: Idea conception and refinement of prompts.
